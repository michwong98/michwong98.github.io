Item Crafting Simumlator

Transmutation Orb
Transform a normal item to a magic rarity.

Alteration Orb
Rerolls the affixes of a magic item.

Augmentation Orb
Adds a second explicit mod to a magic item.

Alchemy Orb
Transform a normal item to a rare rarity.

Chaos Orb
Rerolls the affixes of a rare item.

Regal Orb
Transforms a magic item to a rare rarity..

Exalted Orb
Adds an additional explicit mod to a rare item.

Divine Orb
Rerolls the numerical values of explicit mods.

Orb of Scouring
Transforms a non-unique item to normal rarity.

Orb of Annulment
Removes a random explicit mod.

Blacksmith Whetstone
Increases quality of weapons.
